Discover Lanus

Big city with the culture of a town

Lanús is a densely populated industrial suburb just south of Buenos Aires, known for its passionate football club and its busy commercial corridors.

While fiercely proud of its industrial identity and football club, Lanús also has a strong musical heartbeat. It's a breeding ground for rock nacional and cumbia bands, with a vibrant live music scene in its many bars and cultural centers. The rhythm of the city is a mix of rock anthems and cumbia, blasting from local clubs. Also share some street and roots with Tango!
