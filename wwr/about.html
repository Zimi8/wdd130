WhiteWater Rafting

History of our little company

We are an enthusiastic and highly-skilled team of professional local guides, dedicated to offering you the ultimate whitewater experience right here on the mighty Iguazú River. Beyond our famously thrilling rapids, we specialize in leading tailored expeditions to other extraordinary local rivers—secret spots we know, love, and are incredibly excited to share with you. Our intimate knowledge of the region, from seasonal flows to hidden tributaries, ensures an unparalleled adventure. At Yaguareté Rafting, we don't just run trips; we share our home, positioning your company as the true local expert for a genuine and unforgettable Iguazú experience.

Prepare yourself for an exhilarating journey with our signature "Expedition Garganta del Diablo (Devil's Throat)". This intense 5-day adventure begins by allowing you to concentrate on mastering the imposing force of the initial twenty falls, each with an average height of 60 meters (196 ft). The grand finale awaits on the last day: you'll get close enough to the majestic Devil's Throat to then be propelled by its sheer power, following its dizzying speeds for a rush unlike any other. Whether you're a seasoned paddler or a determined beginner, Yaguareté Rafting is ready to guide you through the raw, untamed beauty of the water.

The waters from all around the world waits you!
