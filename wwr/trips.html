Discover the heart of the Iguazú River

Classic "Vencejo de cascada"

Our most popular excursion. Two days of adrenaline-fueled adventure beneath the Iguazú River Falls. Enjoy the water like the beautiful native birds Known as Vencejos, who loves the natural shower of the magnificient Iguazú Falls. Perfect for beginners eager for thrills..

  • Dificultad:Moderate
  • Duration: 2 days / 1 night
  • Price: $350 USD

Expedition Garganta del diablo (Devil's throat) (5 Days )

Concentrate the first few days on getting used to the imposing force of the first 20 falls, with an average height of 60 meters. (196 ft) On the last day, you will get close enough to the majestic Devil's Throat to then be propelled by it, following its dizzying speeds.

  • Diificulty: Extreme - DANGEROUS - ALMOST DEADLY
  • Duration: 5 days + 1 night to rest and party.
  • Price: $1000 USD + state permission or donation for convervational efforts

Peaceful rafting

Escape the crowds! A peaceful drift down the Iguazú River with stops to enjoy the lush nature and observe the exotic tropical birds.

  • Difficulty:Easy
  • Duración: 1 Day or 2 days
  • Price: $120 USD
